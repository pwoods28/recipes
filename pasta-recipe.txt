Ingredients
1 large brown onion, finely chopped
100g hot salami sliced,
1 long cut rasher bacon, dice
1 large chicken breast, diced
2 cans of crushed/diced tomato
2 baby chilis- hot, finely chopped
500g pasta, spirals!

Directions
Brown the onions in pan with some oil. Keep heat on high
Add bacon and stir thoroughly for 5 minutes.
Add salami, chili and chicken breast and continue to stir to avoid burning.
Once chicken is cooked, add tomomato and allow to simmer.
Bring large pot of water to boil, add salt and pasta and allow to cook until satisfied.
